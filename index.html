About

Intergalactic Information Incorporated is the premiere GIS consulting firm across the galaxy! We specialize in providing a complete suite of accurate GIS services for clients of any origin. No planet is too big or too small for our team of specialists.

A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away our company was founded. Incorporated on star date 5928.5, Interplanetary Information was founded to meet the vast spatial analysis needs of past and future galaxies both near and far. We are connected by our values of inclusivity and committed to meeting the diverse needs of our clientele across the Milky Way and into infinity and beyond.

Services

Deep Spatial Analysis

Space can be a dangerous place, and it's important to understand the risks. Our deep space analysis toolset can identify risks using our patented Quasar radio detection system. With proper precautions, supermassive black holes won't be an issue anymore! You may lose your planet, but at least you'll know when!

Planetary Site Analysis

Catastrophic planetary collapse caused by incidental changes to the atmospheric layer is unfortunate, but we have all been there. Let us take care of finding the next viable location for your colony so you can relax and plan your new terraforming mission. Our Remote Sensing analysts can image and review all possible land masses and contents wherever you are or may want to go! Don't hesitate to ask us how this can benefit your long-term chance of survival. Whether you want to get ahead of your inevitable planetary destruction while you can, investigate a current crisis or uncover the history on your planet we're here for you!

Cosmos Cartography

More than mapping the stars, each planet comes with its own set of spatial challenges. Our Cosmos Cartographers are here to aid in the process. What can mapping do for you? We can create general reference maps, specialised maps and present strategies for risk analysis and planning. Looking to complete the spice run in under 12 parsecs? We can map it for you. Hoping to rebuild an entire civilization? Our galaxy maps are the place to start!
