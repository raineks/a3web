Frequently Asked Questions

Who is accepted as clients?

At Intergalactic Information Inc. We specialize in producing GIS services to off-world clients! Any citizen that is a member of the United Federation of Planets is welcome!

Can you do analysis of XYZ Planet?

At Intergalactic Information Inc. we believe in a data-first approach. If the data is available, we can do analysis on any planet in the galaxy. Where the data is not readily available, we can work together to construct the database and begin surveying.

Do you take on clients who are light-years away?

Absolutely! We have reliably engineered holographic technologies. We're with you, every step of the way.

Is your data compatible with our technology?

We use the most robust and versatile LCARS Access 441 Data Node 188 that seamlessly integrates and adapts to your local OS.

Can you handle a project with quasars?
