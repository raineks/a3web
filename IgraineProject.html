Cartographic Risk Assessment

Kashyyyk Habitat Mapping

The Planet of Kashyyyk's Environmental Assessment Sector requested consultation to prepare for Wookie habitat risk analysis in the event of a galactic war. By gathering and analysing spatial data, the process of mapping all viable land and developed locations was possible. Allowing further risk planning to begin. By collecting land use, soil type, and population data, our cartographers were able to map all suitable land areas for prominent tree growth and soil density for safety. Additionally providing a basis to work with out Deep Space Analysis unit to find new planets for possible migration should the need ever arise.

The Planet of Kashyyyk, image captured using remote sensing techniques. To learn more about our Remote Sensing services check out our work on Tatooine!
