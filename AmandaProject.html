Space Cat Tracking

After generations of speculation that cats were not "of earth", our team's groundbreaking technology enabled us to track cats back to their origins in the Judgement Galaxy.

Our expedition to what early astrononmers called "The Great Lap in the Sky" uncovered definitive proof of early cat civilization. Fossilized bowls of rejected, perfectly adequate food, long strings, withered leaves from a foyer and several dead earth spiders were strewn about the surface of the planet "Drop It!". The sunniest cliffside location included well-defined imprints of relaxed feline bodies and intriguingly, the one carpeted section of "Drop It!" contained numerous fossilized expulsions.

With endless "waste surface", sanitation is key in dusty habitats! Our accurately mapped facilities help keep your planet pristine.
